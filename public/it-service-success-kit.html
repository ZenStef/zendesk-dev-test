IT Service Success Kit

Keeping your customer data secure can mean very different things to different companies, depending on how many customers you have and what types of data your customers share with you. Watch this 30-minute webinar where Zendesk’s VP of Security, Ryan Gurney, will walk you through best practices for keeping your customer data secure and your team informed. He’ll also outline which features are most important for you based on your business, whether you’re a small business just starting out, or a larger enterprise with a dedicated IT team. And if security is not your forte, no worries - you may be surprised at just how easy these features are to put in place.
